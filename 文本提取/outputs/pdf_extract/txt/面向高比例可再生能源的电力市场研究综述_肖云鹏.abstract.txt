Electricity market acts as the driving force and
platform for promoting the full accommodation of renewable
energy and efficient operation of energy internet. In recent
years, along with the increasing proportion of renewable
energy in electricity market, many problems emerge in
electricity markets of different countries. In this work, based on
the development reality of electricity market adapting to
renewable energy, incentive policies for integrating renewable
energy and trading mechanisms are discussed, special issues
concerning the participation of renewable energy in electricity
market are analyzed, the latest results regarding the electricity
market towards high proportion of renewable energy are
summarized, and key issues for further research and developing
electricity market towards high proportion of renewable energy
in China are proposed.
